Retract-A-Wheels

Overview

The project aimed to design a solution for Ms. Paula Redmond, a stroke survivor who struggles with balancing her bike during initial acceleration. The goal was to help her ride independently and confidently without altering her normal biking experience. The team, consisting of Jayant Khandelwal, Charlie Kalousek, Ryan Murphy, and Lucas Nadeau, worked under the guidance of the Design Thinking and Communication Program at Northwestern University.


Role:

Product Designer
