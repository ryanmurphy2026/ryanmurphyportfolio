Music Taste Analysis

I listen to a lot of music. Like over 189,000 minutes of music over the past two years. I love music so much that every day since 2023, I choose one song to represent that day and put it on a playlist. As of writing, I have picked over 800 songs. This simple habit has become a huge part of me and these songs are very personal to me. After learning about data visualization techniques, I thought it would be cool to analyze my music taste and see how it has changed. Using tools like Exportify and the Last.fm API I turned my Spotify into a spreadsheet to analyze.

First I analyzed how many artists I’ve listened to. Overall, there are 627 unique artists on this playlist. I then chose to analyze the top artists with the cut off being 6 songs.

Similar to artists, I also analyzed how many genres I’ve listened to. Overall, there are 304 unique genres on this playlist. I then chose to analyze the top ten genres.

Due to there being so many niche genres, I decided to categorize every song into one of 12 genre categories and then calculated the percentage of genres for each month.
